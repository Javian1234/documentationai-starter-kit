## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub account for advanced integrations

</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Follow these steps to register, set up your account, and publish your initial content. The entire process takes under 5 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up for Fixit Jamaica" icon="user-plus">

    Visit [https://app.fixitjamaica.com/signup](https://app.fixitjamaica.com/signup) and create your account.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">

        Enter your email, choose a strong password, and verify your inbox.

      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Google" icon="google">

        Click "Sign up with Google" for instant access.

      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    After verification, you'll land on the dashboard.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Project" icon="plus">

    From the dashboard, click **New Project**.

    1. Enter project name: `My First Docs`
    2. Select template: `Documentation Site`
    3. Click **Create**

    Your project opens in the editor with a welcome page.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Initial Content" icon="upload">

    Replace the default content with your own. Here's a sample MDX homepage:

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX">

    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Welcome to My Docs
    description: Your custom documentation site.
    ---

    ## Quick Overview

    Get started with Fixit Jamaica in minutes.
    ````

    </CodeGroup>

    Save changes with `Cmd` + `S` (Mac) or `Ctrl` + `S` (Windows/Linux).

  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate and Edit" icon="settings">

    Use the sidebar for navigation:

    | Section     | Purpose                  |
    |-------------|--------------------------|
    | Pages       | Manage your MDX files    |
    | Themes      | Customize appearance     |
    | Settings    | Project configuration    |
    | Preview     | Live site preview        |

    Edit any page inline, add components like `<Callout>`, and preview instantly.

    <Callout kind="tip">

      Use the search bar to quickly find pages or components.

    </Callout>

  </Step>
</Steps>
