---
title: Create Your First Documentation Page
description: A short guide to creating your first documentation page in Fixit Jamaica Documentation.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your project">
    From the dashboard, click on your project name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Page'">
    Find the 'Add Page' button in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter page details">
    Provide a page title and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Click 'Save' then 'Publish' to make the page live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
